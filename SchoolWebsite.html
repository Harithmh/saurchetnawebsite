About Us

A registered voluntary organisation working for inclusion, rehabilitation, and social welfare.

SPECIAL FEATURE IN BRIEF

SAUR CHETNA EVAM VIGYAN SHODH SANSTHAN is a voluntary organisation working in the field of SUN FOR ALL, including pollution-free awareness, de-addiction, rural development, and welfare of specially abled persons.

The institution is registered under Section 28 of Rajasthan State Registration Act 1958 with Registration No. 139/SGNR/90-91. It is also registered under 12-A and 80(G) of IT Act, and under PWD Act 1995 and JJ Act 2000 and 2006.

The organisation is affiliated with the Social Justice and Empowerment Department of Rajasthan and the Education Department of Rajasthan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for parents, guardians, and supporters.

Who can take admission in Seva Ashram-1?

Seva Ashram-1 supports specially abled children requiring inclusive and residential educational support. Families can contact us directly for eligibility guidance.

What services are available at Seva Ashram-2?

Seva Ashram-2 provides M.R. home and rehabilitation support with care routines, vocational activities, and wellbeing-focused guidance.

Our Services

Holistic services designed to improve learning, independence, health, and dignity for every child.

📚

Special Education

Personalized learning plans for children with intellectual, hearing, and visual challenges through inclusive classroom strategies.

Focus area: foundational literacy, life skills, and confidence-building with child-wise progress tracking.

🛠️

Rehabilitation Services

Structured rehabilitation and vocational pathways through practical activities, guided routines, and capacity-building modules.

Includes training in crafts, basic digital literacy, and productivity-based activities for independence.

🏠

Residential Care

Safe and supervised residential and day-care support with structured daily routines and compassionate guardianship.

Campus support includes hygiene, safety, nutritious meals, and a child-friendly environment.

🥗

Nutrition & Daily Care

Balanced nutrition, hygiene guidance, and daily care protocols designed to improve physical wellbeing and readiness to learn.

Regular monitoring helps maintain healthy routines and supports better concentration in classes.

🏃

Co-curricular Activities

Sports, yoga, drills, and creative indoor activities that encourage physical fitness, discipline, and emotional resilience.

Students participate in structured group sessions to build teamwork and social confidence.

🏥

Clinic Services

Preventive health checks, specialist referrals, and periodic health records for sustained child development.

Integrated support includes wellness monitoring and counselling inputs whenever required.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Counselling

Family counselling and awareness sessions to help parents build strong, supportive home environments for children.

Practical home-care guidance is shared to continue progress beyond the classroom.

🎓

Staff Development

Continuous teacher and caregiver training to keep the institution aligned with modern inclusive education practices.

Capacity building is strengthened through workshops, peer learning, and sector conferences.

Our Objectives

Our goals are practical, measurable, and centered on dignity, inclusion, and long-term empowerment.

Build an inclusive and addiction-free social environment

We promote social awareness and responsible community participation so every child can grow in a safer and more inclusive ecosystem.

Deliver integrated education and rehabilitation support

Education, vocational training, rehabilitation, and health-linked support are coordinated to improve daily functioning and life outcomes.

Create meaningful opportunities for specially abled children

We focus on opportunities that strengthen confidence, communication, skills, and independent participation in society.

Enable social integration and family participation

Our programs connect school, family, and community to help children transition into wider social and educational spaces.

Run awareness and counselling for sustainable impact

Parents and caregivers receive guidance to continue positive support at home and reduce long-term barriers.

Encourage research and welfare-driven initiatives

We support surveys and field learning to improve child welfare planning and community-level support systems.
